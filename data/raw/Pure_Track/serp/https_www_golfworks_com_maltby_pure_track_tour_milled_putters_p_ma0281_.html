Description

The Maltby PTM Pure-Track Tour Milled putter features a re-designed modern blade putter profile specifically designed to increase accuracy and distance control using modern head dimensions and weights. These modern specifications increase MOI vs. traditional milled putters and allow for greater customization via the use of counterbalanced weights, custom club lengths or heavy weight putter grips.

The Tour Milled putters are meticulously manufactured from premium S25C Japanese Carbon Steel blocks. The S25C blocks are then 100% CNC milled to produce exact spec weights, spec tolerances and aesthetics. The heads are then plated using a satin gloss TIN Plating process to protect the carbon steel material.

PTM-1 Blade Highlights

  • 100% CNC milled from premium Japanese S25C Carbon Steel for unprecedented specification and aesthetic quality
  • Classic shape re-designed with a modernized profile dimensions and weight to increase MOI (4762 g-cm²) for a significantly more forgiving putter head increasing distance and accuracy
  • Flared 1.75” hosel provides ¼” offset which is the preferred transition from shaft to leading edge of most players
  • Horizontal CNC face milling improves roll consistency
  • PTM-1 Blade putter requires a Flared Tip Putter Shaft (MA0310)
  • Available in RH and LH options
  • Headcover MA0328 included
  • Note: This is a club head only and not an assembled golf club
