Technology-Enabled Transformation

Data Transformation & Analytics

Generate and operationalize intelligent data insights at scale   

For CFOs, CIOs, and corporate leaders to cost-effectively achieve strategic growth, real-time data insights, competitive differentiation, and complete compliance, they need a robust digital architecture that maximizes technology investments and process efficiencies. Our data systems specialists design, build, and deploy leading data, analytics, and automation programs that generate rapid ROI across the enterprise. 

Create future financial value today. Here’s how.

Unlock the value of organizational data with a holistic data strategy

A data strategy brings clarity to where data lives, who owns it, how it relates to the business, how to make decisions based on the data, and how data can be used for greater value creation. We establish data strategy programs that:   

  • Govern and promote data management, corporate policy, and business goals with agility  
  • Uncover critical internal control, regulatory, and risk management needs  
  • Establish continuous data quality monitoring and remediation 
  • Democratize data by establishing data literacy and an innovation culture 
  • Leverage modern cloud platforms and data models to enable proactive insights 
Learn more about our Data & Analytics Framework
Migrate to a modern data architecture to enable a streamlined flow of data to stakeholders

As organizations grow organically or through acquisition, so does the volume of data consumed and needed to sustain growth. By investing in cloud-based platform solutions, organizations can collect, store, transform, process, and analyze data in a single source of truth consistently, efficiently, and without siloes. The modern data stack allows organizations to cost-effectively scale and unlock value to users by democratizing access and usability of data. Our experts: 

  • Assess the current data architecture and recommend a future state 
  • Set up your data warehouse and pipeline/integration development for source system data 
  • Transform your data and database architecture  
  • Enable role-based security configuration 
  • Configure live reporting architecture with direct connection to business intelligence tools 
Get fast, accurate insights to enable reporting and decision-making with advanced business intelligence capabilities

A robust business intelligence and data analytics program drives optimized decision-making, elite business performance, and strategic risk management. By allowing you to analyze data, generate meaningful reports, identify patterns, predict outcomes, and visualize results with speed and regularity, you can confidently make informed, forward-looking decisions. Our experts:  

  • Design and operationalize flexible, scalable analytics programs 
  • Unlock, accelerate, and centralize data assets
  • Design reports and dashboards to empower executive decision-making
  • Help you understand multi-faceted data sets to empower users to develop strategic insights and storytelling
Maximize efficiencies and empower your people to add more value through automation and analytics

Create process efficiencies, improve controls, reduce errors, and promote compliance through advanced automation and analytics programs. To free up staff for value-add activities and to ensure success, we:   

  • Establish and operationalize a center of excellence to govern, scale, and maximize ROI 
  • Prioritize processes fit for automation, select vendors, and lead the development and implementation of pilot, proof of value, and turnkey production-ready bots  
  • Enable a culture of continuous improvement with effective change management and citizen development 
  • Surface decision-ready insights through integrated analytics platforms 
Access trusted partnership and support along each stage of your data evolution with managed data services

Wherever your organization is on its data transformation journey, our managed data services team provides advisory expertise and dedicated staff to implement and maintain your infrastructure, model, and analytic solutions as defined by your goals. We:  

  • Establish the right level of governance, security, and policy to support your desired maturity level 
  • Advise on, implement, and manage the architecture that enables your data solutions to run smoothly 
  • Develop your analytic and business insights solutions with hands-on professionals to maintain and support
