    Description

    A UNIQUE WEATHER FORECAST VISUALISATION
    While most weather forecasting apps rely only on free global models, Windy has gone a few steps further and invested heavily in the most accurate models available, always at the highest possible resolution, to ensure accurate weather forecasts.

    Available weather models: ECMWF (9 km resolution), HRRR, NAM, GFS, ICON-D2, ICON-EU, ACCESS, AROME, NEMS

    WEATHER RADAR & SATELLITE IMAGERY
    A weather radar is the most requested tools for weather forecasting. To provide the most accurate radar imagery, we are constantly expanding our resources so that rain, snow, and storms don't catch you by surprise.

    MORE THAN 50 WEATHER MAPS AND TOOLS

    Wind (current speed, gusts, accumulation)
    Temperature, humidity, pressure, dew point, freezing altitude
    Rain, thunder, storms, precipitation type, CAPE-index
    Weather radar and Satellite images (blue, infrared, visible)
    Clouds, clouds tops, clouds base and visibility (LIFR, IFR, MVFR, VFR), fog
    Waves, swell, currents
    Solar activity maps (solar power, UV index, wet-bulb temperature)
    Snow depth, new snow, snow density
    NO2, PM 2.5, Aerosol, SO2, Ozone layer, Surface ozone, CO concentration, Dust mass, Fire intensity
    Weather warnings & Extreme forecast
    Soil moisture, Moisture anomaly, Drought intensity

    WEATHER ALERTS BASED ON YOUR PREFERENCES
    Want to know about essential weather forecasts before everyone else? Set-up a custom alert based on weather in your favorite location. Whether it's wind strength, precipitation, or temperature.

    HURRICANE TRACKING, STORM TRACKING
    With Windy's hurricane tracking tool, you can track a hurricane's past and projected path. Windy doesn't rely on just one model for hurricanes but will give you all the forecasts available for a given storm or hurricane.

    WEATHER WIDGETS
    Windy serves up a quick weather overview on your iPhone or iPad, as well as live radar. You can choose from several view sizes and types. 3-5 day forecast, live radar, live satellite, or a simple lock screen overview.

    TOOLS MADE FOR PILOTS
    16 altitude levels from the surface to 13.5km/FL450
    50k+ Airports searchable by ICAO and IATA, including runway info, decoded & raw METARs, TAFs and NOTAMs
    Detailed Airgram and Meteogram
    Thermals (beta)
    Meteogram: temperature and dew point, wind speed and wind gusts, pressure, precipitation, altitude cloud cover

    Brilliant App

    I first heard about Windy from someone who suggested using it with fire alerts to track where the wind would blow bushfires through the days ahead. If they will be blown towards my house I take my dog out with me for the day. For a while this was all I used it for - and thought it particularly well designed and easy to use.
    I told friends about it. Some of them got back to me and mentioned other features they found. One friend gets concerned about thunderstorms and finds the weather radar option reassuring as it shows that a “very close” lightening strike was actually more than 5 km away. 😊
    I have the active fires option switched on and can see which countries have adequate bush fire resources - and which, just over a border, do not. Also when Ukraine and Gaza have fires caused by bombing (corresponds to news reports).
    There is a richness of features, details very well presented, easy to use - I am happy to rate Windy 10 out of 10.

    baddpix , 27/12/2024

    Excellent tool for aviation

    Windy is an outstanding resource for pilots in general aviation. It greatly improves situational awareness, because it is so much more detailed and so much more visually accessible than standard meteorological prediction products. The number of prediction layers is staggering - if you want to know about clouds, you can ask about low clouds, cloud base, cloud layers, cloud ceiling, overall cloud cover, freezing level, thunderstorm potential, etc etc. You can interrogate the atmosphere at any height using a slider, and look at temperature, wind speed and direction, etc etc. For student pilots and newly qualified pilots, Windy is very useful for learning more about practical aviation meteorology and for helping pilots to stay out of trouble. The 10-day forecasts and the 1-hour forecasts available in the pro version are extremely useful for forward planning.
