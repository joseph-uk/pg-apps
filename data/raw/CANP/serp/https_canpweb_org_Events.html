Members in Motion

Connect with colleagues, strengthen your practice, promote your profession, and impact legislation by participating in CANP-hosted events throughout the year. Our Annual Educational Conference, Lobby Day, Leadership Summit, House of Delegates session and local chapter events all present excellent opportunities to take an active role in advancing your career and influencing the direction of CANP.

CANP Annual Educational Conference

Our signature can't-miss event of the year

Experience the largest gathering of nurse practitioners on the West Coast as more than 600 NPs and related health care professionals convene to learn, exchange ideas and explore the latest products and services for our profession. Visit our comprehensive Annual Educational Conference page to learn about the upcoming conference and to register.

Power In Practice

The California Association for Nurse Practitioners (CANP) provides a forum and unifying voice for more than 30,000 nurse practitioners statewide, often joining forces with other state and national organizations, to advance the profession and bridge health care needs. As the only association solely dedicated to advocating for nurse practitioners in California, we work to protect and expand the critical roles filled by NPs. As a conduit of information, we foster the dissemination of ideas, advice and standards of practice.
