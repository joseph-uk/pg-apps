CANP SMU Alumni Mixer

Gather with your fellow alums for complimentary hors d'oeuvres and a drink at the Westin Rancho Mirage Golf Resort & Spa.

Please register before attending, and we will see you at the CANP Annual Educational Conference!

 

 

Event date & time:

Thursday, March 20, 2025

6:30-7:30pm

Location:

Masters Plaza at The Westin Rancho Mirage Golf Resort & Spa in Rancho Mirage, CA
