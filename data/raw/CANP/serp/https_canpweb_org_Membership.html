Power in Practice

Across the State, but Always Together

When you join the California Association for Nurse Practitioners (CANP), you become part of a proud, united community of advanced practice nursing professionals, NP students and other supporting health care organization members. CANP empowers its members by fostering their professional development, promoting them as integral leaders, and educating the public on the skills and capabilities of nurse practitioners.

Join CANP View Your Benefits

Community

Find inspiration from a supportive network of peers.

Advocacy

Add to CANP’s collective voice at the State Capitol.

Opportunity

Access events, education, legal advice, and discounts tailored to NPs.

MEMBERSHIP

Invest in you. Join CANP today.

Becoming the best NP you can be takes strength and determination. Find both within our supportive network and look no further than your local CANP chapter for opportunities to connect with like-minded NPs in your own community.

Join CANP Renew Membership

Benefits

Not convinced yet? Check out these great member benefits.

Attend the Annual Educational Conference

It’s the don’t-miss educational and networking event of the year. Our annual conference is California’s only event focused exclusively on continuing education for advanced practice nursing.

Ask a Practice Question

Have a question about your medical practice? Our “Ask a Practice Question” feature is one of our most valuable benefits, allowing you to get timely answers from knowledgeable and respected nurse practitioners selected by CANP leadership.

Ask a Legal Question

Need expert legal advice related to your practice from someone you can trust? CANP offers up to three free legal consultations per year with a CANP attorney who is also a licensed nurse practitioner.

Find a Job

Get leads on great jobs with competitive salaries through the Job Center, our one-stop resource for focused online job searching.

Build Your Local Network

Engage with nurse practitioners in your area. CANP’s network of chapters throughout California offers valuable support, interaction with other NPs and educational opportunities through monthly meetings and special events.

Limit Your Liability

Power In Practice

The California Association for Nurse Practitioners (CANP) provides a forum and unifying voice for more than 30,000 nurse practitioners statewide, often joining forces with other state and national organizations, to advance the profession and bridge health care needs. As the only association solely dedicated to advocating for nurse practitioners in California, we work to protect and expand the critical roles filled by NPs. As a conduit of information, we foster the dissemination of ideas, advice and standards of practice.
