Annual Educational Conference

Our Signature Can’t-Miss Event of the Year 

Supported by NSO

March 19 - 22, 2025 | Westin Rancho Mirage Golf Resort & Spa

Register Today!

Don’t miss this unique opportunity to connect with peers, advance your knowledge, and experience the best of what our profession has to offer. Whether you’re a seasoned practitioner or new to the field, the CANP Annual Educational Conference provides valuable insights, skills, and connections that will benefit your career and practice.

ATTENDEE REGISTRATION

SPONSOR/EXHIBITOR REGISTRATION
 

Event Highlights 

Join the largest gathering of nurse practitioners (NPs) on the West Coast at the breathtaking Westin Rancho Mirage Golf Resort & Spa, near Greater Palm Springs. More than 600 NPs and health care professionals will convene to learn, exchange ideas, and explore the latest products and services in our field. From Wednesday, March 19, 2025, to Saturday, March 22, 2025, immerse yourself in three days of education, networking, and relaxation at this premier destination retreat. 

We're excited to announce Dr. Bernadette Melnyk, PhD, APRN-CNP, FAANP, FNAP, FAAN will join the 2025 conference as the Annual Educational Keynote Speaker.  Renowned both nationally and internationally, Dr. Melnyk has a distinguished career as a leader in nursing and health care innovation.  She is one of very few women and nurses elected to the Institute of Medicine – one of the highest honors in the fields of health and medicine. During her career, Dr. Melnyk founded the National Interprofessional Education Practice Collaborative to advance the U.S Department of Health and Human Services Million Hearts initiative.

Networking 

CANP’s Annual Conference offers extensive opportunities for NPs to meet, collaborate and learn from fellow colleagues.  From seasoned professionals to those up and coming in the profession there’s a place for everyone to learn and network.  Join us at any of the myriad of gatherings throughout the multi-day conference to make sure you’re connecting with the best and brightest.  One of the key highlights in 2025 will be the Friday night event taking place March 21 from 7-10pm at the Westin Rancho Mirage.  Come enjoy music, entertainment food & drinks and more.  We’ll see you there!

Power In Practice

The California Association for Nurse Practitioners (CANP) provides a forum and unifying voice for more than 30,000 nurse practitioners statewide, often joining forces with other state and national organizations, to advance the profession and bridge health care needs. As the only association solely dedicated to advocating for nurse practitioners in California, we work to protect and expand the critical roles filled by NPs. As a conduit of information, we foster the dissemination of ideas, advice and standards of practice.
