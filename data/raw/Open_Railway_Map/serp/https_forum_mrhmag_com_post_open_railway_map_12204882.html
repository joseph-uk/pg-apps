If you want something added...

You may need to do it your self. This is a "crowd sourced" database. Open Street maps is that also. Keeping any large area map current is a major undertaking. By getting interested people to make the updates a lot can get done in a short time. In the past I have submitted revisions to the Open Street Map.

Temporal (historical) data would be wonderful! Again interested people will have to find a way to add it. Maybe a separate effort from the current Open Railway Map. 

The three places I know well enough to look at with a critical eye need more work for the current  rail lines.
