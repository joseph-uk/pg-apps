About us

The leading independent rail and coach travel platform selling rail and coach tickets to millions of travellers worldwide. Via our highly rated website and mobile app, people can seamlessly search, book and manage their journeys all in one place. We bring together millions of routes, fares and journey times from more than 270 rail and coach carriers across 45 countries. We offer our customers the best price for their journey and smart, real-time travel information on the go. Our aim is to make rail and coach travel easier and more accessible, encouraging people to make more environmentally sustainable travel choices.

    More exciting news for Trainline customers in Spain 👇

    Jody Ford Jody Ford is an Influencer
    2mo

    I enjoyed an excellent series of meetings with policy makers and journalists in Madrid last week, and was proud to front up our latest announcement in person: at Trainline we are now selling tickets for "Cercanias", the commuter trains that operate in 12 Spanish cities and carried 450m passengers last year. Trainline is the only retailer that has integrated these commuter tickets into a single platform alongside Spain's high-speed and mid-distance rail services. Not only will this attract new audiences to Trainline, but it also underlines our credentials as Spain's one-stop online shop, making it easier to buy train tickets - whatever the journey and whoever the operator.

    👇 "The value of competition between different carriers cannot be overstated – it improves choice, standards rise, prices become more competitive, and ultimately more people choose the sustainable option of rail." 💚

    Jody Ford Jody Ford is an Influencer
    2mo

    Super event in Brussels on Monday, bringing together rail industry leaders, academics and politicians to discuss how the EU can maximise the benefits of competition in rail. The value of competition between different carriers cannot be overstated – it improves choice, standards rise, prices become more competitive, and ultimately more people choose the sustainable option of rail. And I’m proud that Trainline plays a major role in growing the market by unlocking all the options for passengers. We simplify the complexity and make it easier for people to choose the right service for their needs. Thanks to all the attendees for their passionate contributions and ongoing determination to make rail more attractive, more accessible and more affordable. Next stop Barcelona, then Madrid - and I'll be taking full advantage of competition with a choice between four different carriers that link the two cities!
