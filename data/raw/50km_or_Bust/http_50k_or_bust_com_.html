The Book
50K Or Bust! aims to present the early cross country pilot with the basics of cross country paragliding in an easily understood form based on techniques required for Britain's temperate island climate.

Print On Demand Publishing
Books are individually printed to each customer's order and usually delivered in about a week in the UK. The content can be updated almost at will enabling the author to respond quickly to feedback from readers. Much has been clarified and added since 50K Or Bust! was first published.
