Paragliding cross country flying is my passion!

My name is Arnaud Baumy, and I offer private paragliding cross-country courses.

I grew up near Annecy and started flying in 1994 when I was 14. Today, I am a certified instructor since 2004 and also coach of the Rhône-Alpes Auvergne Hope League team. With over 30 years of experience, especially in distance flying, I’ve had the chance to fly all over the world.

To keep progressing, I regularly train in cross-country and competition flying. My best result? 3rd place in the Paragliding World Cup (PWC) in 2017!
Among my most memorable flights are a journey from La Grave to the top of Mont Blanc and a 260 km triangle from Bourg-Saint-Maurice.
My way of working is slightly different as the regular schools which offer collective cross country courses. I mostly offer private trips and coaching ( 1 to 1 or with small group ). I love working that way as I think it’s much more efficient to transmit my passion and knowledges. There are also much more human exchanges as it’s easier to get to know each other.

Through XC Guide, I organize cross-country paragliding courses to share my passion.

My approach is a bit different from traditional schools: I focus almost exclusively on private lessons. In my opinion, this is the best way to share my passion and everything I’ve learned. It also fosters a deeper connection of sharing and exchange.

I fly alongside you as much as possible, either solo or in tandem with a partner of your choice, offering guidance and helping you bring out your best.

Whether you’re flying solo or with a small group of friends, I design a custom paragliding experience just for you. You choose the date and the flying area, and I take care of the rest: accommodation, logistics, everything is covered.

My goal? For you to enjoy the experience fully, stress-free.

My courses are entirely tailor-made. If the weather isn’t ideal, no worries—I can offer you some mini-wing flying or a hike-and-fly adventure. There are plenty of options!

My courses are open to all levels, from complete beginners to experienced pilots. The flights and goals are tailored to your skill level.

My priorities: fly safely, have fun, and help you progress!

I usually organize cross-country flying trips in areas I know best: the Northern Alps (Savoie, Haute-Savoie, Isère) or further south near Saint-André-les-Alpes. But we can also head further, like to Spain or Italy.

In winter, I teach speed riding at Les Arcs. More info at www.speedriding-school.com.
