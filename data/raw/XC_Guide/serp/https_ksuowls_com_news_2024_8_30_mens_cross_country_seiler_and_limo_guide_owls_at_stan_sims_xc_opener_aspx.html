Seiler and Limo Guide Owls at Stan Sims XC Opener

8/30/2024 12:26:00 PM

By: Hunter McKay

Story Links

Men's Results | Women's Results 

ACWORTH, Ga. – 23 members of the Kennesaw State men's and women's cross country teams competed at the Stan Sims Cross Country Opener at Allatoona Creek Park on Friday morning.
 
The KSU women and men both took home third place.
 
In the men's four mile run, freshman Brian Limo posted a time of 19:22.75 to finish fourth and pace the Owls. Diego Fernandes crossed the line in a time of 20:28.10 to take home 27th. Matthew Hall ran a time of 20:39.65 to secure 31st. Noah Sybert (20:59.78) and Grant Crabtree (21:11.23) rounded out the scoring for the Owls finishing 40th and 42nd.
 
The women were led in the 5k by Madison Seiler who ran a time of 17:48.12 to place fourth. Natalie Cummings ran a time of 18:10.26 to place 12th. She was followed closely by Madigan Wallace who finished in a time of 18:13.99 to place 14th. Emma Sullivan finished 18th with a time of 18:36.26. Anna James rounded out the scoring for KSU placing 24th with a time of 18:48.01.
Quotable
Head Coach Adam Bray
 
On Today's  Performance …
"It was great to get the season underway this morning at the Stan Sims Opener. I thought both teams competed really well for where we are in our training currently. I'm looking forward to the next few weeks of improving our fitness and sharpening up our racing skills. CUSA and beyond is our goal but this was a great start to our season."
 
On Brian Limo and Madison Seiler …
"Congrats to Brian Limo and Madison Seiler for earning top 5 individual finishes!! It was great to see two Owls battling to win."
 
Next Up
The Owls will travel to Huntsville, Ala. for the Southern Showcase hosted by Jacksonville State on Saturday, Sept. 13.
