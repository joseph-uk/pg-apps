XCTrack

XCTrack is an Android app for PG & HG

XCTrack covers everything you need:

  • valid and reliable IGC tracklog
  • easy one-click upload to XContest
  • XC navigation with FAI assistant, thermal assistant and online progressive optimization
  • full-featured XC competition navigation
  • map view with terrain map
  • airspace view and warnings, automatical update of airspace activations
  • supports many external devices
  • localized to many languages
  • fully customizable
