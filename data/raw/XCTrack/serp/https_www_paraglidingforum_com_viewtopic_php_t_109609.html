With all respect... yes, when you move the phone over the vertical axes, it correctly reverses becase the phone is pointing to a different direction, isn't it? It seems to me that this is actually returning the results as intended.

I had the same problem and the way to fix it is: never use "INTERNAL COMPASS" as the internal compass of any mobile phone needs calibration very often. If you DO NOT check the "internal compass" box, then your direction will be computed with your movement and GPS, hence it will always work well
