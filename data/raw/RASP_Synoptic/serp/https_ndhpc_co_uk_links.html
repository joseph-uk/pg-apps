Other South West Clubs

———————————

We have reciprocal flying rights with the other clubs in the southwest – full members of the NDHPC can fly the sites of the clubs listed below, and we welcome members of these clubs to fly with us on the north coast.

Devon & Somerset Condors: www.dscondors.co.uk
