Model 2400

24-Track Flagship Analog Recording Console & Live Mixer with Audio Interface

22 channels, 24 tracks, one solution - from performance to recording, Model 2400 re-defines what you can do with a studio recording and live mixing console.

Streamline Your Mix Process with Advanced Onboard Processing

The Model 2400’s versatility doesn’t compromise its mixing capabilities one bit. With per-channel compression, multiple sub groups, and master stereo bus processing including compression and EQ, you’ll have the pro-level mix control you need in any scenario. Taking your project from tracking to a final master on the Model 2400 couldn’t be simpler.

22 Channels and 24 Tracks, One Solution

With comprehensive 22-channel mixing capabilities, the Model 2400 is an ideal studio and live mixer for everything from larger combos and jazz ensembles to orchestra and choir ensembles. And when it comes to recording, no additional interface or computer are required.

Insert Professional Processors and Effects

Insert points on channels 1-12 allow you to easily integrate a studio rack full of processors and effects units. And, in addition to the onboard Master Bus processing, a send/return loop on the Main output allows you to connect external mastering gear such as compressors, EQs, stereo imagers and sonic exciters.

Ample Aux and Sub Outputs

With 5 Aux sends and 4 stereo sub groups, Model 2400 handles advanced mixing duties with ease. Create more monitor mixes without sacrificing an aux for effects. Connect multiple pairs of studio monitors for checking your mixes. In your studio or on stage, Model 2400 delivers the comprehensive connectivity and routing you need.

Professional Recording Capabilities

Model 2400 seamlessly integrates multitrack recording with studio and live mixing. With the ability to record 24 channels (22 inputs plus the stereo master) of high-quality audio direct to SD card media, Model 2400 is a powerful recording solution even if you’re a DAW-less producer.

Additional Features

Seamless DAW Integration and Transport Control

The Model 2400 streamlines your workflow even when you’re using it in a typical studio environment with a computer-based DAW. The onboard 24x22 USB audio interface eliminates the need for external interfaces and converters. And thanks to dedicated transport buttons linked to your DAW, you can keep your hands on your console instead of a mouse.

Sound with Excitement

TASCAM Ultra HDDA (High-Definition Discrete Architecture) mic preamps give Model 2400 the vibrant analog sound that makes harmonics come alive.

With Ultra HDDA, any sound source will be represented with the utmost clarity and richness, and with an ultra-low noise floor.

TASCAM initiatives for high-quality mic preamps

Monitor Your Signal Virtually

With the Model Mixer Settings Panel app, users can take advantage of a digital meter bridge across by connecting the Model 2400 via USB to a PC or Mac. Set peak hold times, monitor the input, MTR or USB signal all while being able scale the GUI from a small window to full screen. Take it a step further by porting the app from your computer to a dedicated monitor of your choosing.

Ready to Be Your Studio Centerpiece

Model 2400 delivers the classic analog console experience in the studio, with an onboard 24x22 audio interface that’s ready to meld with modern production workflows. Insert points on channels 1-12 allow you to integrate studio processors and effects. And with the ability to return 22 DAW tracks through Model 2400’s mix path and processing, it’s also the most versatile analog summing mixer you’ll find.

MIDI Interface

Model 2400’s comprehensive MIDI functionality allows full integration with MIDI-based production setups. From controlling drum machines and synthesizers from your DAW software, to synchronizing external effects processors to the tempo of your music and creating click tracks, Model 2400 offers the MIDI functionality you need to make it happen.

 

Features at a Glance

 

Key Features
  • 24-track multitrack recorder using SDXC SD card (punch-in/out supported)
  • 24 in / 22 out USB audio interface
  • 22 analog input channel modules with 16 XLR Mic Pre-amps, utilizing TASCAM ultra HDDA Mic Pre-amp technologies and a one knob compressor (Ch 1-12)
  • Equipped with input inserts (SEND/RETURN) on channels 1 - 12
  • 5 Aux (4 Aux plus internal FX) sends, 4 stereo sub group and Main L/R
  • MIDI in/out allows USB MIDI interface and MIDI sync, 1/4" audio click output with tempo control
  • DAW transport control and REC READY control via HUI/MCU
  • Dedicated Settings Panel application with Meter bridge

 

Other Features
  • Selectable send Pre/Post EQ routing for Recording
  • Ch compressor and EQ bypass function available for MTR and USB return
  • Phantom Power (+48V) selectable ingroups of 4 channels
  • 100Hz Low-cut Filter (Ch 1-12)
  • 3 Band EQ (Ch 1-12: sweep-able mid-range frequency)
  • Dedicated 100mm fader (Ch 1-22, stereo Sub1-4 and MAIN L/R)
  • Internal 4 band EQ and master compressor on Main bus
  • Equipped with inserts (SEND/RETURN) on Main bus
  • PFL/AFL function with LED indication in all input / output modules
  • MAIN outputs L/R via XLR balanced; TRS ¼" outputs via Sub 1-8, Aux 1-5 and Control Room output
  • 2 Phone outputs
  • Talkback XLR microphone input
  • 16 TASCAM on-board studio quality Effects such as reverb, delay, chorus with fader control
  • Bluetooth® input supporting both AAC and SBC codecs
