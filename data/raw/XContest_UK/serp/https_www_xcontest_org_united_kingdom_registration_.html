Registration

The registration is mandatory for participation in the contest.

The username can contain lowercase and capital letters, alfa and numeric characters. Spaces are not allowed.

All fields marked with asterisk (*) are mandatory. Please entry date of birth in form "dd.mm.yyyy" (e.g. 28.02.1976).

After successful registration you will receive confirmation e-mail.

For existing XContest users:

If you are already registered in the World XContest, you have not to register as a new user for the XContest UK!

Sign in under your current XContest account and confirm here your registration data.
