Cintas Center
Xavier's 19 intercollegiate teams compete in a number of on and off campus venues. Cintas Center is arguably one of the best on-campus arenas in the nation and is home to basketball and volleyball. The Heidt Family Champions Center at the O'Connor Sports Complex opened in 2021 and is home to soccer, lacrosse, baseball, cross country and track and field.
