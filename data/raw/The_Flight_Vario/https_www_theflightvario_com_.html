The first vario app for paragliding engineered around the Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) to detect motion in all directions. The IMU centric design allows for a vario app outstanding in both, Â precision and robustness. This is achieved by continuously cross checking all sensor readings from the accelerometer, gyroscope and magnetometer with the barometric sensor. In contrast to varios and apps built around the barometer only, false indications of lift are minimized and the feedback is instant. Centering the core of a thermal is more intuitive and natural. What you hear is what you feel - a zero lag vario with no thermal missed! A vario that beeps you up where you belong!

Our smartphone varios range from a Basic Vario indicating your altitude and climb rate while flying over to a more Classic Vario that logs your flights and calculates speed over ground, direction of flight and above ground level information while paragliding. Our top of the line XC Vario comes fully packed with multiple altimeter settings, airspaces, waypoints, thermal maps and live tracking. The XC Vario App will also run conveniently in the background with screen turned off for hours and hours. This is useful if you are low on battery, just need the beep or want to use your smartphone as backup vario or logger.
